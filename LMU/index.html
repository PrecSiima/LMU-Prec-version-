Welcome

Welcome to the official website of LIFE Ministry Uganda, a ministry of Campus Crusade for Christ International. We thank you for setting aside your time to visit us online. You will get to know about the ministry we do, and you are welcome to be part of what we do in relation to specific areas of your interest. The welcome remarks from our National Team Leader, Dr. Dickson Obwoya give a general picture of LIFE Ministry Uganda and the links to various ministry departments, staff and activities provide specific information that you may need to know. What is Life Ministry Uganda?

LIFE Ministry Uganda is an affiliate of Campus Crusade for Christ, an inter-denominational Christian organization currently operating in over 200 countries worldwide. From its humble Beginning in 1951 when Dr. Bill Bright responded to Godâ€™s vision to start a ministry of evangelism and discipleship among university students at University College of Los Angeles, the organization has grown from two local staffs (Bill and Vonette Bright) to more than 21,000 international staff worldwide.
